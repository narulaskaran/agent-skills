# Gut Health Troubleshooting

Systematic approach when user reports bloating, constipation, or reflux.

## Trigger

User reports bloating, constipation, acid reflux, or any gut discomfort related to diet.

## Diagnostic Flow

1. **Load 3-5 days of food logs** from the agent's food-log directory
2. **Scan for high-gas vegetables:** kale, cauliflower, broccoli, brussels sprouts, cabbage (cruciferous/brassicas ferment heavily)
3. **Scan for fried food frequency:** deep-fried items (chicken 65, etc.) slow gastric emptying
4. **Check water intake:** plain water rarely logged explicitly, but infer from: coffee (diuretic), alcohol, soda vs water mentions. Psyllium (Metamucil) + chia seeds demand extra water.
5. **Check dairy frequency:** yogurt, milk, cheese. South Asian populations have high lactose sensitivity.
6. **Check meal timing:** food after 9PM sits undigested when lying down → reflux + slow motility
7. **Check alcohol:** any amount dehydrates and slows gut motility
8. **Check fiber-to-water ratio:** fiber supplements (Metamucil, chia) without water = cement

## Reflux-Specific Triggers

These relax the LES (lower esophageal sphincter) or are highly acidic:

- Caffeine (coffee, pre-workout, energy drinks)
- Alcohol
- Chocolate
- Mint
- Spicy food
- Tomato-based foods
- Citrus/acidic fruit (oranges, grapefruit, lemon, pineapple)
- Raw onion/garlic
- High-fat meals (slow gastric emptying)
- Carbonated drinks

### Reflux-Safe Vegetables (low-acid, low-gas)

Banana, melon (honeydew, cantaloupe, watermelon), cucumber, celery, carrots, sweet potato, potato, spinach (cooked), zucchini, green beans, asparagus.

### Reflux-Triggers to Avoid

Citrus, tomato, pineapple, raw onion, raw garlic, peppers (bell + chili), cruciferous raw.

## Bloating + Constipation: Common Patterns

| Pattern | Likely Culprit | Fix |
|---------|---------------|-----|
| Veggies present but bloated | Cruciferous veg (kale, cauliflower, broccoli) | Swap for cucumber, zucchini, carrots, cooked spinach |
| Taking fiber supps + constipated | Not enough water | 3L+ water/day minimum with psyllium |
| Late-night eating + morning bloat | Food undigested in horizontal position | No food after 9PM |
| Daily yogurt + bloating | Dairy sensitivity | Drop dairy 48h test |
| Restaurant meals dominant | Hidden oils, fats, low water | Prioritize home meals; restaurant oil 2-3x what you'd use |
| Alcohol + next-day bloat | Dehydration + slowed motility | Extra water before bed after drinking |

## Mechanics (Non-Food Fixes)

- Stop eating 3 hours before bed
- Walk 10-15 min after meals (gravity + movement = motility)
- Elevate head of bed 6-8 inches (blocks under posts, not pillows)
- Smaller meals, don't stuff in one sitting

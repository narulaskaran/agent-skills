# Lob Postcard Print Specifications

PostalForm routes postcard orders through Lob. These are Lob's standard print specs for postcards.

## Paper Stock

| Postcard Size | Stock | Weight | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| 4×6 | 14pt cover | ~300 GSM | Lighter, mailable as postcard |
| **6×9** | **16pt cover** | **~350 GSM** | Thick, substantial feel |
| 6×11 | 16pt cover | ~350 GSM | Same stock as 6×9 |

16pt = 0.016" thickness. Durable enough for USPS transit, thin enough to be a recognizable postcard (not a slab).

## Printing

- **Process:** Full-color digital (CMYK)
- **Front:** UV-coated for gloss finish and mail-handling protection
- **Back:** Uncoated/matte — PostalForm prints address block here

## Color Accuracy

Lob uses ChromaChecker standards and GRACoL 2013 for color consistency across their print network.

## Quality Notes

- 1/8" precision trimming
- Glue and UV coating for transit protection
- No paper stock selection options — fixed per size

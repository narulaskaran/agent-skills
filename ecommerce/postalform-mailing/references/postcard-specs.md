## General Rules

- 2-page PDF: Page 1 = front (artwork), Page 2 = back (mailing side)
- Do NOT place addresses, indicia, or barcode on PDF — PostalForm fills mailing block
- Extend background/artwork to document edge (bleed area)
- Keep all text within safe zone (0.125" from trim line)
- `mailpiece_type: "postcard"` with matching `postcard_size`
